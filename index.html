Sustainability at our heart

We are working to embed sustainability practices into every element of Eat. Drink. Think., from the venue to the food and fixings.
The Garden Museum is a centre of horticultural and environmental education and pioneers sustainable community initiatives in the heart of London. Built from a church with the addition of sustainable materials and solar panels, it was one of the first buildings to meet Lambeth Council’s ambitious target of a 40% decrease in carbon emissions.
We are also minimising printed collateral, and will use recycled paper and sustainable sources for ink and print, with carbon neutral delivery to and from the site.
